Types of AI:

1. Narrow or Weak AI: Designed to perform a specific task, like facial recognition, language translation, or playing chess.
2. General or Strong AI: A hypothetical AI that possesses human-like intelligence, reasoning, and problem-solving abilities.
3. Superintelligence: Significantly more intelligent than the best human minds, potentially leading to exponential growth in technological advancements.

AI Applications:

1. Virtual Assistants: Siri, Google Assistant, Alexa
2. Image and Speech Recognition: Self-driving cars, security surveillance
3. Natural Language Processing (NLP): Chatbots, language translation
4. Predictive Maintenance: Industrial equipment monitoring
5. Healthcare: Medical diagnosis, personalized medicine
